About Coffee Bliss

Where every cup tells a story of passion, quality, and community

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Coffee Bliss began with a simple dream: to create a space where people could experience the art of coffee brewing at its finest. What started as a small corner café has grown into a beloved community hub.

Our journey has been guided by an unwavering commitment to quality and sustainability. We source our beans from ethical farms around the world, ensuring every cup not only tastes exceptional but also supports fair trade practices.

Today, Coffee Bliss is more than just a coffee shop – it's a place where friendships are formed, ideas are born, and happiness is brewed daily. We're proud to serve our community and look forward to welcoming you into our story.
